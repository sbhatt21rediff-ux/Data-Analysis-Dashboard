# Sales Dashboard Analysis (Interactive Dashboard on MS Excel)

## Project Overview

This repository contains an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel.The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of total sales performance, regional insights, product analysis, and customer behavior.It visualizes key business metrics and helps identify trends, high-performing categories, and areas for improvement.

## Dataset used
https://github.com/sbhatt21rediff-ux/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/raw/main/Sales_Data_Analysis.xlsx

## Analytical Questions Derived from the Sales Dashboard
1. Which region demonstrates the highest total sales performance, and how does it compare with other geographical regions?
2. Which product sub-category contributes the most to overall revenue, and how does it compare in terms of quantity sold?
3. What is the comparative performance between Online and Offline sales channels in terms of total sales contribution?
4. Which delivery status (Delivered, In-Transit, Cancelled, Returned) accounts for the largest portion of total sales, and what insights can be drawn from this distribution?
5. Which payment mode (Card, COD, UPI, Wallet) is most preferred by customers, and how does it impact sales volume?
6. Which month of the year records the highest sales performance, and what seasonal or temporal patterns can be observed?
7. Which city contributes the maximum to total sales, and how does its performance compare with others?
8. How does sales performance vary across different product categories such as Beauty, Clothing, Electronics, and Home Décor?
9. What relationship exists between net sales and quantity sold across product sub-categories?
10. Which specific regions or product categories exhibit lower sales performance, indicating areas for business improvement?

## Process Involved
-Collected and organized raw sales data in Excel (Order ID, Category, Region, Sales, etc.).
-Cleaned and formatted data — removed duplicates, corrected categories, added calculated fields like Net Sales.
-Created multiple Pivot Tables to summarize sales by region, status, and category.
-Built interactive visuals using Pivot Charts (Column, Combo, Treemap, Pie).
-Added Slicers for dynamic filtering (Region, Category, Order Status).
-Applied professional formatting, consistent colors, and clear labels.
-Finalized and published the dashboard for interactive Excel analysis.
